About FitLife Gym

Empowering lives through fitness since 2010

Our Story

FitLife Gym was founded in 2010 by fitness enthusiasts John and Sarah Thompson. What started as a small, local gym has grown into a thriving community of health-conscious individuals. Our journey has been driven by a passion for helping people transform their lives through fitness.
