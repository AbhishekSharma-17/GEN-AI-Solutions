Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the gym access?

Gym access includes use of all our state-of-the-art equipment, including cardio machines, weight training area, and functional fitness zones.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year. A small fee may apply.

Is there a joining fee?

We occasionally run promotions with no joining fee. Check with our staff for current offers.

How do I cancel my membership?

You can cancel your membership by giving 30 days notice. Please speak to our front desk staff or log in to your online account.
