Our State-of-the-Art Facilities

Experience fitness at its finest with our modern equipment and amenities designed to support your wellness journey.

Cardio Area

Our spacious cardio area features state-of-the-art equipment to help you achieve your cardiovascular fitness goals.

Features:

  • Latest treadmills with built-in entertainment systems
  • Ellipticals with adjustable incline
  • Stationary bikes with virtual riding experiences
  • Stair climbers and rowing machines

Weight Training Area

Build strength and sculpt your body in our comprehensive weight training area.

Features:

  • Wide range of free weights and barbells
  • Smith machines and power racks
  • Cable machines for versatile workouts
  • Dedicated areas for functional training

Group Exercise Studio

Join our energizing group classes in our specially designed studio spaces.

Features:

  • Spacious studios with sprung floors
  • High-quality sound systems
  • Adjustable lighting for different class atmospheres
  • Equipment for various class types (yoga mats, steps, weights)

Swimming Pool

Dive into fitness with our indoor swimming pool, perfect for lap swimming and aqua classes.

Features:

  • 25-meter indoor pool with lane dividers
  • Heated water for year-round comfort
  • Dedicated times for lap swimming and classes
  • Poolside relaxation area

Sauna and Steam Room

Relax and recover in our sauna and steam room facilities.

Features:

  • Traditional Finnish sauna
  • Steam room with eucalyptus infusion
  • Cool-down area with refreshments
  • Towel service available

Locker Rooms

Freshen up in our clean and well-maintained locker room facilities.

Features:

  • Spacious changing areas with private cubicles
  • Secure lockers with digital locks
  • Modern shower facilities with complimentary toiletries
  • Grooming stations with hair dryers

Experience Our World-Class Facilities

Join FitLife Gym today and gain access to all of our state-of-the-art facilities. Whether you're looking to build strength, improve cardiovascular fitness, or relax and recover, we have everything you need to support your fitness journey.
